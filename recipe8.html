Pineapple Casserole
Ingredient Quantity
Canned pineapple chunks, drained with juice saved 2 (20-ounce Cans)
Sugar 1/2 cup
All purpose flour 6 tablespoons
Shredded sharp cheddar cheese 2 cups
Ritz crackers (crushed) 1.5 sleeves
Butter (melted) 1/4 cup
Instructions
1: Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and spray a 2-quart baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. Drain the pineapple very well and reserve 6 tablespoons of the juice for the recipe.
2: In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour and 6 tablespoons of the juice. Add the pineapple and cheese and mix well. Pour it into the prepared dish. Sprinkle a tad more cheese over the top.
3: In a medium bowl, add the melted butter to the crushed cracker crumbs and stir to coat them in the butter. Sprinkle them evenly over the cheese. Bake for about 30 minutes and then allow the casserole to rest for about 10 minutes and thicken before serving.
