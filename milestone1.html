Milestone 1

Ballantyne Beans Cafe

  • What is the purpose of the site?

This site will host and deliver information about this buisiness to customers. It will help inform the customers about menu items, locations, availability, history, etc.

  • What do you want the web site to accomplish?

I would like this site to answer as many questions as possible for customers in regards to the business. Secondly to inform them about the company and about the history and science of coffee.

  • What opportunity, problem, or issue is your site addressing?

In this era I feel every business needs to have a website to be competitive and relevant. It could be for answering simple questions about hours and locations or even serve as a method of marketing.

  • Who is your intended audience?

Anyone who drinks coffee. More specifically the working commuting public, students, or anyone that would enjoy visiting a coffee shop.

  • What type of content might be included in your site?
  • Menu
  • Hours
  • Locations
  • Pricing
  • About
  • History
  • Reviews
  • Examples of similar sites.
